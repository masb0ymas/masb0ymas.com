### Description
Seadev is a specialized talent acquisition platform connecting innovative startups and established tech companies with top-tier engineering talent across Southeast Asia. The platform streamlines the recruitment process by providing access to a curated network of pre-vetted software engineers, designers, and product specialists, enabling companies to build high-performing technical teams efficiently while offering competitive opportunities to skilled professionals in the region.
